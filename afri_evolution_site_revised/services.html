Frequently Asked Questions

Can we start with one layer?

Absolutely. Each layer is designed to deliver value independently. You can begin with the one that addresses your most pressing need and add others over time.

Do you integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. We work with Webflow, Power Platform, WhatsApp Business API, major CRMs, analytics tools, and Microsoft 365—designed for the African SME tech stack.

How does AI fit into these systems?

AI is embedded throughout: copy optimization in Launchpad OS, forecasting in Clarity Engine, workflow suggestions in Flow Studio, lead scoring in Connect CRM+, and multilingual conversations in ChatBoost AI. All human-supervised and explainable.

What about data privacy and security?

We follow consent-aware data handling, role-based access controls, audit trails, and POPIA compliance. Your data stays in your environment where possible, with transparent governance documentation.

How do we scope a first project?

Start with a Discovery workshop where we map your goals, processes, current tools, and KPI targets. We'll recommend the best entry point and integration roadmap.

What does handover include?

Complete documentation, recorded training sessions, admin access, governance playbooks, and optional ongoing support subscriptions.

Is this suitable for African businesses?

Yes—that's our focus. We optimize for low-bandwidth environments, support multilingual needs, integrate with WhatsApp Business, and understand local business contexts and compliance requirements.
