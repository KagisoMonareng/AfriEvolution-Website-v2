Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We primarily partner with ambitious small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and mid-market businesses across diverse sectors. Our clients are typically those with some existing technological infrastructure and data, actively seeking to leverage AI and automation for enhanced efficiency, cost reduction, and competitive advantage.

How do you ensure measurable ROI for our business?

Our solutions are meticulously designed with measurable outcomes in mind. We conduct initial, detailed assessments to identify specific pain points and quantifiable opportunities. We then implement solutions with clear Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as operational cost reduction, time savings, lead conversion rate improvements, or increased customer satisfaction, allowing you to track your tangible return on investment.

What is "AI-as-a-Service" (AIaaS) and how does it benefit us?

Do we need to have existing tech infrastructure to start?

Not necessarily! Our "Digital Foundation" package is specifically designed for businesses just beginning their digital journey, helping you establish the necessary foundational infrastructure. For our more advanced "Automation Essentials" and "AI Advantage" packages, some existing digital presence or data is beneficial, but we always begin with a thorough assessment to meet you precisely where you are and design a suitable roadmap.

How does Afri Evolution handle data privacy and security with AI solutions?

We prioritize data privacy and security with an "Ethical & Compliant AI by Design" approach. All our solutions adhere strictly to relevant regulations like POPIA in South Africa and global best practices for data governance. We ensure transparency in data usage, implement robust security measures to protect your information, and maintain a commitment to responsible AI deployment to build and maintain trust.
