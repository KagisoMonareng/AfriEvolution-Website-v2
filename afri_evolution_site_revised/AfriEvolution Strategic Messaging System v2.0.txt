# 1) Executive Summary

**Who we are.** AfriEvolution is an **AI Transformation Partner** for growth-oriented SMEs. We build **AI-powered business systems** that combine low-code automation with applied intelligence—delivered with human clarity.

**What we offer.** A modular, interoperable **five-layer stack**:

1. **Launchpad OS** (digital presence),
2. **Clarity Engine** (data & forecasting),
3. **Flow Studio** (process automation),
4. **Connect CRM+** (relationships & pipeline),
5. **ChatBoost AI** (conversational engagement).

**Why it matters.** SMEs don’t need more tools; they need **systems that learn**. Our stack turns fragmented processes into a **single operating rhythm**—reducing manual work, improving decisions, and accelerating growth.

**For whom.** **Tech-lite teams** who want AI and automation **without hiring developers**. Leaders who value control, clarity, and measurable outcomes.

**How we charge.** Hybrid model: **setup + subscriptions** (AIaaS). Each layer stands alone; together they compound value.

**Promise.** *Clarity engineered.* Systems that people trust and AI that quietly multiplies output.

# 7) Product System Messaging — The AfriEvolution Stack

## 7.1 Launchpad OS — *Digital Presence Layer*

**One-liner:** Your brand’s **operating surface**—fast, credible, analytics-ready.
**What it does:** High-performance website/CMS with AI copy assist and UX scoring.
**AI:** Copy optimization, component suggestions, SEO tuning.
**Why it matters:** Converts attention into qualified interest; foundation for data capture.
**Pricing:** Setup (R35k–R60k) + support (R1.5k–R2k / mo).

## 7.2 Clarity Engine — *Data Foundation Layer* (formerly DCF)

**One-liner:** Your **single source of truth** with predictive insight.
**What it does:** Unified model, pipelines, dashboards, anomaly/forecasting.
**AI:** Auto-schema builder, anomaly detection, small-data forecasting.
**Why it matters:** Decisions based on shared, trusted data.
**Pricing:** Setup (R20k) + subscription (R1.2k–R2.5k / mo).

## 7.3 Flow Studio — *Workflow Layer*

**One-liner:** **Automate the repetitive** so people focus on the valuable.
**What it does:** Approvals, notifications, document generation, scheduled jobs.
**AI:** “FlowSense” suggests automation and SLA improvements.
**Why it matters:** Time back to teams; fewer errors; visible SLAs.
**Pricing:** Setup (R25k–R50k) + per-workflow (R600–R1.2k / mo).

## 7.4 Connect CRM+ — *Relationship Layer*

**One-liner:** **Client clarity**—contacts, pipeline, tasks, and insights in one place.
**What it does:** Unified CRM, reminders, email/WhatsApp logging, simple forecasting.
**AI:** Lead scoring, churn risk, sentiment tracking, next-best action.
**Why it matters:** Better follow-through, higher retention and win rates.
**Pricing:** Setup (R15k) + per-user (R500–R1.5k / mo).

## 7.5 ChatBoost AI — *Engagement Layer*

**One-liner:** **Conversational AI** that qualifies, books, and supports 24/7.
**What it does:** WhatsApp + web chat, trained on your knowledge; CRM + calendar + payment hooks.
**AI:** Multilingual LLM, retrieval from Clarity/CRM, intent routing.
**Why it matters:** Faster responses, more leads captured, better NPS.
**Pricing:** Setup (R12k) + usage tiers (R1k–R2.5k / mo).
